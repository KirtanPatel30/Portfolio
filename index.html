An end-to-end data project spanning Data Engineering, Analytics, and Machine Learning on 114,000 Spotify tracks across 113 genres. Built a complete pipeline from raw CSV to an interactive analytics dashboard — with a unique angle on Indian vs global music.

PythonPandas SQLiteSQL Scikit-learnPlotly Dash MatplotlibSeaborn

  • Dashboard: Interactive 5-tab Plotly Dash app with genre explorer, audio feature radar, top artists leaderboard, and Indian vs global comparison.
